About Me

Hello Y'all! My name is Belen Szentes and I am a student at Rice University ('25) with a Major in Mechanical Engineering and a Minor in Engineering Design.

I am passionate about designing devices around the user and really getting to understand the point of view of the consumer. I have taken 2 years off of school to intern throughout a variety of industries. I started off in the Med Tech sector (J&J, Cala Health) and moved into Consumer tech (Apple) and even the Automotive industry (Tesla). So far, my internships have yeilded one released product, the 6th Generation iPad Air, and one published patent for a wearable device. I am super excited to see where my career journey takes me next!
